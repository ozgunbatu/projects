Electric Vehicle Demand Forecast – Germany

The rapid adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) has created regional inventory imbalances. Some cities experienced overstock, while others faced shortages. Using historical sales and registration data, we optimized allocations and improved forecast accuracy.

Data sources: Historical EV sales & listings (CSV), ZSW Electromobility – Regional registrations (CSV)

Problem Definition

Hamburg, Berlin, and Munich exhibited significant inventory imbalances. For example, Hamburg had 20 vehicles in stock while 50 orders arrived, creating overstock costs, whereas Berlin had 10 vehicles for 25 orders, leading to potential shortages. Our analysis aimed to optimize stock allocation regionally.

Data & Methodology

  • Historical EV sales & listings dataset cleaned in SQL (2.3M rows → filtered 200 relevant vehicles).
  • Regional registrations from ZSW to validate forecast and allocation.
  • Power BI dashboards used for visualization, scenario simulations, and interactive planning.
  • Forecast errors reduced by comparing predicted vs actual monthly demand.
