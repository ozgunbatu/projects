EV Demand Forecast – Germany

Optimized regional inventory and forecast accuracy using SQL, Power BI & data-driven simulations.

← Back to Portfolio

Problem

Overstocking in some regions and stockouts in others led to operational inefficiency. Management required a predictive model to optimize inventory allocation across Germany.

Approach

  • Collected 2.3M transaction rows from AutoScout24 & ZSW Electromobility.
  • Cleaned & aggregated data in SQL; performed seasonal trend analysis.
  • Implemented Power BI dashboards to simulate regional inventory allocation.
  • Forecast vs actual demand visualization and interactive scenario analysis.
