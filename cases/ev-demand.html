EV Demand Forecast – Germany

← Back to Cases

Problem

Over-stocking in some regions and stockouts in others led to operational inefficiencies, increased holding costs, and missed sales opportunities. Management needed a data-driven forecast model to optimize inventory allocation across Germany.

Approach

Collected 2.3 million transactional rows from AutoScout24 and ZSW Electromobility. Performed SQL cleaning, region-wise demand analysis, seasonal trend identification, and stock allocation simulations.

Recommendation

Implement a region-specific inventory allocation dashboard, updated weekly, enabling operations teams to proactively manage stock levels and reduce both overstock and stockouts.

Implementation

Developed a Power BI dashboard with:

  • Interactive slider to simulate monthly demand changes per region
  • Visual comparison of forecast vs. actual demand
  • Heatmaps for stock level alerts across 6 regions

Results / KPIs

  • Inventory reduced by 7%
  • Forecast error decreased by 22%
  • Decision-making speed improved by 15%
  • Optimized regional allocation across 6 key German regions
