EV Demand Forecast – Germany

Optimizing regional inventory and forecast accuracy using SQL, Power BI & scenario simulations.

← Back to Portfolio

Problem

In Hamburg, for example, stock levels were 20 vehicles while orders reached 50. This caused additional procurement costs, delayed deliveries, and reduced customer satisfaction. Similar imbalances existed in other regions: excess inventory in some, insufficient stock in others.

The challenge: how to optimize inventory allocation across regions to minimize costs and meet demand accurately.

Data Sources & Cleaning

  • AutoScout24: EV sales & listing data
  • ZSW Electromobility: Regional EV registration & trend data
  • Total dataset: 2.3M rows (cleaned with SQL: duplicates removed, nulls fixed, dates normalized)

Approach & Recommendations

  • Analyzed historical sales per region to forecast demand.
  • Created region-wise simulation in Power BI to optimize inventory allocation.
  • Implemented scenario testing: e.g., demand ±10%, stock adjustment slider.
  • Provided weekly operational updates to ensure real-time decision-making.

Implementation

Power BI dashboard displays:

  • Forecast vs Actual demand per region
  • Inventory levels Before/After optimization
  • Interactive sliders for scenario simulation
