Problem

Over-stocking in some regions and stockouts in others caused inefficiencies and lost sales. Management needed a predictive model to optimize inventory allocation across Germany.

Approach

Collected 2.3M transaction rows from AutoScout24 and ZSW Electromobility. Performed SQL cleaning, seasonal trend analysis, and simulated stock allocation per region.
