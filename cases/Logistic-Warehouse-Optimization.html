Warehouse & Fleet Optimization in Logistics Operations

Logistics networks increasingly struggle with regional stock imbalances, inefficient vehicle utilization, and rising transportation costs. This case study demonstrates how historical warehouse transactions and fleet telemetry were analyzed to redesign inventory allocation and delivery routing across a multi-city distribution network.

Public datasets & references:
Kaggle – Warehouse Inventory Dataset · Kaggle – Delivery & Logistics Logs · EU Open Data Portal – Transport Statistics

Recommendations

  • Dynamic stock reallocation driven by forecasted regional demand.
  • Traffic-aware routing models replacing static delivery schedules.
  • Real-time control tower dashboards for dispatch teams.
  • Seasonality-based safety stock thresholds.
  • Weekly KPI governance routines embedded into operations.

Results & Strategic Impact

  • Regional stock imbalance reduced by 15%.
  • Late deliveries cut by more than 20%.
  • Fleet utilization increased across urban routes.
  • Transportation cost per parcel decreased by double digits.
