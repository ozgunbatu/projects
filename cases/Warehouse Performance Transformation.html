Operating Context

The facility is a 45,000 m² omnichannel distribution center serving both B2C and B2B flows. It processes more than 42,000 order lines per day across 18,000 SKUs with semi-automated picking and three operational shifts.

Value Stream

Inbound → Quality Check → Put-Away → Storage → Replenishment → Picking → Packing → Sortation → Shipping

Business Problem

During peak seasons the site experienced growing backlogs, overtime costs and declining customer satisfaction. Executive management launched a Lean Six Sigma initiative to restore service level while controlling cost.

  • Order Lead Time: 19.4h vs 12h target
  • Picking Accuracy: 97.8%
  • Dock-to-Stock: 9.2h
  • Overtime cost +22%
