Market Context - Germany EV Landscape 2025

German EV Market Dynamics: Despite subsidy removal in late 2023, the German BEV market recovered strongly in 2025 with 16.9% market share (EU-wide). BMW successfully navigated the subsidy cliff, posting +41.3% growth in Germany while the overall market grew modestly. Key success factors: Neue Klasse pre-launch momentum, iX1/i4/i5 product strength, and Tesla's competitive stumble (-62.2% in Q1 2025).

  • Subsidy Dependency: German BEV market dropped 15.9% in 2024 post-subsidy removal - need demand resilience

  • Purchase Complexity: Average consideration period extended to 8.2 months (vs. 4.1 months for ICE) - friction in journey
