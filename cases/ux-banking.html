Problem Definition

Mobile onboarding flows faced high drop-offs during identity verification. Camera permission blockers, unclear capture instructions, and fallback inconsistencies caused delays and increased manual compliance reviews.

Recommendations

  • Progressive camera permission prompts with clear guidance.
  • Fallback document-upload channels added.
  • Inline capture overlays implemented.
  • Real-time funnel dashboards introduced.
  • Dynamic fraud-screening thresholds applied.

Results & Next Steps

  • Camera-related abandonment decreased significantly.
  • Manual review workload reduced.
  • Onboarding stabilized under peak traffic.
  • Framework prepared for rollout to other products.
