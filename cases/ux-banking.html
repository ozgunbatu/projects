Problem Definition

High drop-off rates during identity verification and onboarding were observed. Completion rates varied across users, and frustrations were recorded in qualitative interviews.

Data & Methodology

  • User journeys mapped and friction points identified.
  • Figma wireframes & interactive prototypes tested with 42 participants.
  • Feedback coded to highlight recurring pain points.
  • Metrics measured and compared before & after UX improvements.

Recommendations

  • Identity verification steps simplified; inline validation added.
  • Clear progress indicators and error feedback implemented.
  • Iterative prototype refinements validated via user testing.

Results

  • Drop-off reduced by 18%
  • Completion rate increased by 20%
  • Positive usability feedback from all participants
