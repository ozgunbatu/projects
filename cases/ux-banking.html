1. Problem

Rapid growth in digital banking volumes created operational bottlenecks. Identity verification triggered abandonment due to blocked camera permissions and unclear in-app guidance.

2. Analytical Approach

  • 120k+ sessions analyzed using SQL funnel models.
  • Step-level conversion and latency quantified.
  • A/B testing executed across redesigned UX flows.
  • Permission-handling logic simplified.

3. Implementation

  • Progressive disclosure of camera permissions.
  • Fallback upload paths for restricted browsers.
  • Inline validation and instructional overlays.
  • Real-time funnel dashboards in Power BI.
