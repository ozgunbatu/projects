Secure Mobile Onboarding Optimization

Conversion deterioration during identity verification was observed across digital banking onboarding journeys. UX redesign and permission handling adjustments were implemented to stabilize completion rates while preserving regulatory rigor.

Problem Definition

A surge in mobile-first account creation produced localized friction in biometric capture steps. Elevated abandonment, prolonged onboarding cycles and rising manual compliance interventions were recorded.

Data & Methodology

  • 120,000+ anonymized onboarding sessions aggregated from public fintech datasets.
  • Step-level event logs reconstructed in SQL to derive funnel metrics.
  • Latency and permission-denial rates computed per screen.
  • Controlled A/B experiments executed across redesigned UX flows.
  • Statistical significance evaluated at 95% confidence.

Recommendations

  • Progressive permission prompts contextualized to camera usage.
  • Fallback document-upload channels introduced.
  • Inline capture guidance overlays deployed.
  • Real-time funnel dashboards institutionalized.
  • Fraud-screening thresholds recalibrated dynamically.

Results & Next Steps

  • Camera-related abandonment materially reduced.
  • Operational review volumes declined.
  • Onboarding stabilized at scale.
  • Framework prepared for rollout across additional products.
