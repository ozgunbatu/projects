Viral & Referral Mechanics

  • Invite Teammate: Every Professional tier user can invite unlimited colleagues (view-only access). Upgrading to more "editor" seats drives expansion revenue.
  • Shareable Process Maps: Users can generate public links to specific process visualizations - embeddable in presentations, docs. Branded with FlowMetrics watermark ‚Üí awareness driver.
  • Formal Referral Program (Q2 2025 launch): 10% recurring commission for 12 months on referred customer revenue. Target: 35% signup attribution to referrals (currently 18%).
  • Co-Marketing Case Studies: Offer 25% annual discount in exchange for public case study participation - generates SEO content + social proof. 18 customers enrolled so far.
Viral Loop Math: Current viral coefficient K=0.31 (each user invites 0.82 teammates √ó 38% conversion). Target K=0.50 by EOY 2025 through prompting improvements and referral program launch. At K=0.50, every 100 signups ‚Üí 200 total users over 6 months (2x efficiency gain).
